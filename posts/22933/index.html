等位基因特异性表达 Allele-specific expression (ASE)

发表于 2023-05-23 分类于 读文章
关于ASE的一些知识点总结。

https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-biodatasci-021621-122219

主要名词解释

Allelic imbalance: difference in chromatin state or mRNA or protein levels between the alleles at a locus

等位基因失衡： 染色质状态或mRNA与蛋白质水平在同一基因座不同等位基因之间的差异

Allelic expression imbalance: allelic imbalance that results in differences in mRNA levels, from complete imbalance with exclusive expression of one allele to subtle differences in expression between alleles

等位基因表达失衡： 以上情况的等位基因失衡导致的mRNA水平差异，包括完全失衡（aka只表达其中一个等位基因）和两种等位基因表达产物之间微弱的表达差异

Allele-specific imbalance: difference in chromatin state or mRNA or protein levels between the alleles at a heterozygous locus that is due to the genetic differences between the alleles

等位基因特异性失衡： 由遗传差异导致的某一 杂合 基因座上的等位基因失衡

Allele-specific expression (ASE) : allelic expression imbalance that is genetic in origin, i.e., a difference in expression levels between alleles due to differences between the allele sequences

等位基因特异性表达： 由等位 基因序列本身的差异 导致的差异性表达

另一种解释： ASE refers to unequal expression between paternal and maternal alleles of a gene in a diploid individual, driven by cis-regulatory variants located near the gene. (https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pgen.1008786)

通过杂交两个不同品系进行转录组测序来研究ASE的原因（个人理解）

假定父系和母系具有不同的性状，且两个都是经由高度近交导致的纯合品系（比如AA和aa），F1后代就会是杂合Aa，这样一旦有ASE的存在，在F1后代的转录组数据中就能检测出来，然后进一步推断那些存在ASE的位点可能就是和特定性状相关的基因。
